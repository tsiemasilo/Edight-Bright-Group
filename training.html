Training & Empowerment

Building the Next Generation of IT Professionals

Empowering South Africa's Youth

Recognizing South Africa's high youth unemployment rate, EDGAR BRIGHT GROUP is committed to developing the next generation of IT professionals.

Through our comprehensive in-service training and internship programs, we provide hands-on experience and mentorship to students and graduates, bridging the gap between education and industry readiness.

Our programs have successfully placed over 50 trainees in meaningful employment, with an 85% job placement rate. We partner with universities and technical colleges to identify talented individuals and provide them with the practical skills needed to thrive in the technology sector.
